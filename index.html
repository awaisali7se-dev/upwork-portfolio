What I Do

End-to-End Medical Billing Services

From the first patient encounter to the last dollar collected — I handle it all with precision and compliance.

Patient Demographic & Charge Entry
Accurate entry of patient demographics and encounter charges, ensuring every service is captured and billed with zero information gaps.
Insurance Eligibility Verification
Real-time eligibility checks to confirm coverage, copays, deductibles, and prior authorization requirements before services are rendered.
Claim Scrubbing & Submission
Thorough claim scrubbing to catch errors before submission, ensuring clean claims reach payers on time and get processed without delay.
Denial Management & Appeals
Root-cause analysis of denied claims, crafting compelling appeal letters, and implementing workflow fixes to prevent recurring denial patterns.
Accounts Receivable Follow-Up
Aggressive AR follow-up on outstanding balances — prioritizing high-dollar aging claims, tracking payer responses, and recovering lost revenue.
Payment Posting & EOB Reconciliation
Accurate posting of insurance and patient payments with thorough EOB/ERA reconciliation to ensure every dollar is accounted for correctly.
Prior Authorization
Obtaining prior authorizations and referrals from payers for procedures and medications, minimizing delays and avoiding unexpected denials.
Revenue Cycle Management & Reporting
Full RCM oversight with financial reporting — tracking KPIs like collection rates, denial rates, and AR days to drive data-backed improvements.
ICD-10 & CPT Coding Support
Applying accurate ICD-10 diagnosis and CPT procedure codes to claims, reducing coding errors that lead to rejections and compliance risks.
